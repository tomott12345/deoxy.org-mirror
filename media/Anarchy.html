/What It Really Stands For
Anarchism is a political view derived from the Greek αναρχία ("without archons (rulers)"). Thus "anarchism," in its most general meaning, is opposing rulers. All forms of anarchism oppose the existence of a State and favor what they perceive to be voluntary relationships between individuals.—[1]
