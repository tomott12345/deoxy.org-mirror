Ralph Nader's list of accomplishments include:

  • Launching federal regulatory agencies (i.e. the Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA), Environment Protection Agency (EPA), and Consumer Product Safety Administration)

  • Ensuring access to government through the Freedom of Information Act of 1974.

  • Over eight major federal consumer protection laws (i.e. the motor vehicle safety laws)

  • The recall of millions of defective motor vehicles

  • Dozens of effective citizen groups.
