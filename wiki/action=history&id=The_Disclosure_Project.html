    '''The Disclosure Project''' is a non-profit organization, started by [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Steven_M._Greer Dr. Steven M. Greer] in 1993, with the intention of de-classifying and disclosing all information on unidentified flying objects (UFOs) and issues related to extraterrestrial life that the group alleges covert elements of the United States government have classified and kept secret.[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Disclosure_Project]
     
    It has 400 government, military, and intelligence community witnesses testifying to their direct, personal, first hand experience with UFOs, ETs, ET technology, and the cover-up that keeps this information secret.
     
    On Wednesday, May 9th, 2001, over twenty military, intelligence, government, corporate and scientific witnesses came forward at the National Press Club in Washington, DC to establish the reality of UFOs or extraterrestrial vehicles, extraterrestrial life forms, and resulting advanced energy and propulsion technologies.[http://www.disclosureproject.org/]
     
    The event was attended by all major television networks and by all major print media. 250,000 watched on line during the webcast - the largest number ever for a webcast from the National Press Club.
     
    No television network carried the story. One major newspaper printed a paragraph on the event.
    That such a well-attended news conference such as The Disclosure Project of May 9, 2001 received no television publicity and virtually no print coverage speaks to the power of the media bosses in the United States. The event was covered in Europe.
     
    The two-hour video of the event has been shown to millions of people around the world. A copy of the video is available from http://www.disclosureproject.org/
    [http://www.cosmicparadigm.com/DisclosureProject.html]

    == The Mission ==
     
    -To hold open, secrecy-free hearings on the UFO/ Extraterrestrial presence on and around Earth.
     
    -To hold open hearings on advanced energy and propulsion systems that, when publicly released, will provide solutions to global environmental challenges.
     
    -To enact legislation which will ban all space-based weapons.
    -To enact comprehensive legislation to research, develop and explore space peacefully and cooperatively with all cultures on Earth and in space.
    -To introduce [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zero-point_energy Zero-point energy] as a means for the world to obtain "free energy." According to The Disclosure Project, this will help to end World Poverty and famine, among other things.[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Disclosure_Project]
