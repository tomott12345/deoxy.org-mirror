~pastoral~

of or pertaining to shepherds, from latin pastoralis, from pastor - shepherd, also spiritual guide, shepherd of souls

charmingly simple and serene; idyllic

Pastoral: A literary or other artistic work that portrays or evokes rural life, usually in an idealized way.

...but in this DEOXY-Exhibition the 'rural' is extended to 'urban' in some idyllic context, since for us, internet-users, everything from outer hyperspace is rural, including the urban enviroments.

"Photography is not like painting. There is a creative fraction of a second when you are taking a picture. Your eye must see a composition or an expression that life itself offers you, and you must know with intuition when to click the camera. That is the moment the photographer is creative," Henri Cartier-Bresson
