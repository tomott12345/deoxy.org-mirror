Spiros began writing the book in Nepal year 2003 after having found a copy of James Joyce's book Ulysses on a resturant and was inspired to write a magical book. He has thus far rewritten the book from scratch some 10 times, and says that he has gotten closer to the goal every time. The book has had several names in the past, such as Tales Of The City Different and In The Mid Die, but now goes under the name The Rosalixion.++
