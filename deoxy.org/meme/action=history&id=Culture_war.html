    ~~~Imported from [Wikipedia:Culture_war Wikipedia] on 2010-10-24~~~
     
    The ''culture war'' (or ''culture wars)'' in American usage is a metaphor used
    to describe political conflicts based on sets of conflicting cultural values.
    The term frequently implies a conflict between those values considered
    [Wikipedia:Strict_father conservative] or
    Wikipedia:authoritarian and
    those considered
    [Wikipedia:Progressivism progressive] or
    [Wikipedia:Liberalism liberal]. The "culture war" is sometimes traced to the
    1960s and has taken various forms since then.
