    '''Ralph Nader's list of accomplishments include:'''
    <ul>
    <li>Launching federal regulatory agencies (i.e. the [http://www.csrl.org/reports/OSHA.html Occupational Safety and Health Administration] (OSHA), Environment Protection Agency (EPA), and Consumer Product Safety Administration)
     
    <li>Ensuring access to government through the Freedom of Information Act of 1974.
     
    <li>Over eight major federal consumer protection laws (i.e. the motor vehicle safety laws)
     
    <li>The recall of millions of defective motor vehicles
     
    <li>Dozens of effective citizen groups.
    </ul><br clear="all">
