Greetings stinky, I believe what it means is that if you have, in the instance you gave, a "constant low opnion of yourself", then the appropriate route would be: "an analysis of the sensation with especial respect to any unbalanced leaning between the love and the wisdom or the positive and the negative. Then whatever is lacking in the balanced sensation is, as in all balancing, allowed to come into the being after the sensation is remembered and recalled in such detail as to overwhelm the senses".

So if I understand your question correctly, it is not so much that you "...also have a high opinion of yourself and are not aware of it", as much as it means that you bring it about or create it consciously and with your Will, whether or not you were aware of it or not. If this is accomplished, then in a sense that particular catalyst, I believe, can in time approach a balance of sorts.

However, I do not think it means, overall, that you as a person will then be balanced because of this one catalyst being recognized and balanced out. Although, of course I must follow that up with the fact that on a certain level, you are indeed balanced and always have been; and that this is something to always keep in the forefront of your mind. One of the reasons for this is that: "the proper role of the entity is in this density to experience all things desired, to then analyze, understand, and accept these experiences, distilling from them the love/light within them. Nothing shall be overcome. That which is not needed falls away".

And I believe this quote would apply here as well: "The key to balance may then be seen in the unstudied, spontaneous, and honest response of entities toward experiences, thus using experience to the utmost, then applying the balancing exercises and achieving the proper attitude for the most purified spectrum of energy center manifestation"

In other words, "becoming balanced" of course is the goal to strive for, but a goal that will most likely always be just out of reach, at least in this density. But the striving and dedication to such a goal is what slowly 'graduates' you to each successive level.

I hope this helped with some of the answers you were seeking,
