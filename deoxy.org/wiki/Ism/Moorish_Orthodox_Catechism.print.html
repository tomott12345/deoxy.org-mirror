Subpage : Ism > Moorish Orthodox Catechism

The Moorish Orthodox Catechism consists of no rules or dogmas, but only of adherance to the "Five Pillars" of Moorish Science as listed by Noble Drew: LOVE, TRUTH, PEACE, FREEDOM, JUSTICE to which we add a sixth, "Beauty."

—History & Catechism of the Moorish Orthodox Church of America
